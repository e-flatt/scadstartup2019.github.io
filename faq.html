Deliverables

Throughout the week students will be working towards creating products and services based off their business idea. Below are the deliverables required for the final pitch presentations.

1.Digital Slideshow Presentation

Students will wow the judges with a digital slideshow to explain their idea and area of impact that their new product will have.

Business Model

Through this exercise students will think through all the ways their new company will make an impact and generate revenue.

Prototype (Digital or Physical)

Students need to provide an example of their concept through a prototype that they can show the judges, either in digital or physical form.

Extras!

Anything such as posters, vision video, clothing, etc in addition to the required above 3 deliverables are encouraged! Find what fits your passion and skills set to incorporate it into your teams final presentation.

Judging Criteria

You'll have an oppurtunity to present your StartUp to 5 judges who each have access to 15 million "flux-bux". They can invest as much money as they'd like to the companies of their choice. Judges will be reviewing your projects based on the following criteria:
  • Meet all deliverables - Does your team have everything completed listed above?
  • Measurable Outcomes / Real World Impact - How will your product influence humanity?
  • Visual Prototype - Come with an example of your product to show off
  • "Wow" Factor - Amaze the judges! Be unique and think outside the box!
